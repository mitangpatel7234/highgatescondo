About Project

Highgate Living

Highgate Condos is a new condo project by Truman and is currently in pre-construction. The new project will be located at 8 Avenue Northeast and Range Road 285 in Calgary. Highgate Condos is the new master planned community on Calgary’s East side. Highgate Condos will be surrounded by over 13 acres of parks, trails and open space. Highgate Condos will be 4 buildings, 5 storeys each with a total of 336 mixed condo units. Units will be a mix of one bedroom, two bedroom and three bedroom layout. The estimated completion date for Highgate Condos is set for 2028

  • Developer: Truman
  • Expected Deliver: 2027-2028
